Katya Brown was born in Neumarkt-sankt Veit, Germany in 1963, lives and works in Berlin and Karlsruhe.
Katya studied at Academy of Fine Arts, Munich during 1984-88; University for Fine Arts, Hamburg from 1989-91. She awarded DAAD (German Academic Exchange Service) in 1990, grant to Hong Kong in 1991. Since 2001, she has been appointed as the professor of Academy for Fine Arts Karlsruhe.
